Since Chuu's removal, the members are divided into the following subunits:

  • ODD EYE CIRCLE (Kim Lip, Jinsoul and Choerry);
  • Loossemble (Hyunjin, Yeojin, Vivi, Gowon and Hyeju);
  • ARTMS (Heejin, Haseul, Kim Lip, Jinsoul and Choerry);
  • Chuu (Solo);
  • Yves (Solo).

All 12 members stated that the group did not disband and emphasize their desire to return as a full group. However, they can't use the group name freely, which is why they're leaving the possibility of activities as a full group opened, quoting Lip's words.
